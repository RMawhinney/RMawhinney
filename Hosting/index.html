Project 0 Hosting

SUBJECT/TOPIC: Hosting

This subject is doing overtime as both a broad subject and a specific topic we've covered. Below is the specific assignment and below that are the other tangential topics.

For my personal needs as a site runner I would require a large storage server, capable of holding my dedicated art+story files, and one that would allow for upwards of tens of thousands of views per month, idealistically. I would also require a bandwidth that could accommodate that load based upon the idea of a system that updates frequently along the week requiring multiple visits to stay current. Initially, this means my requirements are very low-tier, though with regards to expansion it means I shouldn't settle for a free-to-start site host. With that, my search specifications led me to Bluehost, which starts at only $4.45/month for unlimited sites.They also have a 24/7 WordPress support network, which is considered a useful tool for those going into multiple updates blog-style like myself. Bluehost also supports scalability meaning that were I to wish to upgrade later on, it would be a seamless transition. Based on my needs and beginner status, I think that Bluehost would be an excellent springboard for starting my site.
