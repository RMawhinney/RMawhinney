Project 0 Description

Web Technologies is a broad category that encompasses a variety of subjects. Along this course I have learned a little bit about a lot of topics, broadening my horizons further than I've considered them. With this experience, I am more readily equipped to chart my course into Web Development and where I want my specialties to lie, though I am not ignorant to the necessity to be a jack of all trades. At its core, Web Technologies is mostly about the structures and media we use to interact with one another digitally or offer digital experiences, and how to necessitate those interactions/experiences within the realm of the Web. Through applying what I've learned and seeking more, I aim to master the methods to make a web experience unique and intuitive and guide them where they need to be.


For this project I have broken the study categories into accessibility. There is no direct "right order" to learn everything in so rather than that the organization covers the physical side of creating websites, the creation itself, hosting, advertising, and the Web itself.
