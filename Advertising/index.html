SUBJECT: Advertising

Once one has the skills necessary for creating a website and hosting it, how is it going to stay afloat or profit? You need to be able to promote your content and your creations, so advertisement is a must. Nothing comes free in the industry, and being able to advertise your products and services to the widest Market possible is one of the most priceless skills a web developer can have. To this extent, of the topics we have covered, Search Engine Optimization, HTTPS, and Commerce are the most useful articles I have found from this course. Well typically advertising can be its own department or branch, knowing how to self-promote as a freelancer is also integral information. With Michael being freelancing, I will need to work extra hard in self-promotion and advertisement in order to reach the same quota as other incorporated ventures.
