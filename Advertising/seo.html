TOPIC: SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

Behold, my scattered notes on Search Engine Optimization.

SEO: Process of assessing current on/off page factors of a website and improving these factors to boost rankings in an engine like Google Optimization: Keywords, Meta description, headings, alt tags, inbound links, page titles Traffic goes hand in hand with rankings (which are a result of many factors, like a grade)

On-page factors:

  • Keywords you’re targeting
  • Written content, headings, titles, etc.
  • Page URLs and their structure
  • Internal links, such as footers/nav systems

Off-page factors:

  • Social media
  • Reputable industry sites
  • Blog posts
  • Product review sites

USABILITY
Acquisition+retention overlapped more
Focus: Design/write for customers first and search engines second;
Never sacrifice usability for rankings;
Give visitors direct access to info--don’t bury it;
Focus on proper design techniques

Buying Process Need Recognition->Solution Exploration-> Evaluate options-> Buy-> Evaluations-> Post-purchase Eval. Many places to put advertisement in

Expectations: results vary week to week, and is ongoing. -target a niche -fake until make

Understand Customer: ask users about social media accounts and devices and target the largest demographic

Buyer Persona: the ideal/most common full customer Uncluttered design, focus on usability, quick access, (price insensitive, fast shipping, target industry)

Simple keywords vs long tail -easy, difficult to target vs low competition+search volume

Keyword List for buyer targets, find related searches and narrow down through that, explore competition. QUALITY > QUANTITY Google Keywords helps

Search for each keyword, use SEMrush to id. Top-ranking sites from that keyword, run side-by-side comparison

PAGE TITLES 50-60 characters max. Use important keywords near the beginning Use unique page title for site Description: 150-160 char., easy to understand, unique desc for each page

Page Structure: Headings, copy, and images Most priority in search engines in h1 and h2 Product desc. Uses keywords Images with alt-tags

Internal linking Anchor text: avoid generic text, don’t use images as links, large footers can hold several internal links Persistent navigation: fixed nav bar

Inbound links If possible have third-party sites link to your site using relevant anchor text

Page Rank Checker

Duplicate content: confuses search engines/wont display from dupes so ensure which to choose is clear. Titles should be unique.

“Canonical” tag helps prevent dupes by showing origin pt.

Keyword density: (Keywords/total words)x100 -rough guideline. Natural text>density

DON’T do cloaking: hide keywords in bg colored text
Target a few keywords and use appropriately through site (use headings!)
Use free compression tools to reduce filesize and bandwidth load/reduce load lag
-Provide summary/content about video nearby and offer share links

PageSpeedInsights Optimize vids/pics, Use quality host, minify code, use Content Delivery networks(CDN)
CSS MINIFIER (minifycode.com)
