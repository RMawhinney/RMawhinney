TOPIC: E-Commerce (for WordPress)

Behold, my scattered notes on E-Commerce (for WordPress).

Ecommerce in WordPress Wordpress Essential training is good background for this
Area of interest: Hobbies, passion, industry

Product Types: Physical or Virtual

Physical products: Acquisition, Storage, Fulfilment   Create: large time and money
  Wholesale: resale with markup, harder for startup, but easily scalable
  Drop-shipping: act as third party between product and customer
  Manufacture: time+effort+money

Virtual Products(like books, Software, affiliates[recommending products]):

AUDIENCE

Create Personas: detailed representations of users, and focus on ONE
Persona details: Job, Daily Routine (where would they see this ad?), Experience

Beginners would want to know: why buy here, what materials? Advanced: Brand, social proof

  Gravity Forms and Ninja Forms

  • Grav: All in one, fixed cost; many services
  • Ninja: A la carte, low cost but fewer services

    •   Bundles: Aims at new customers and give them everything they need to start for something
      Add-ons: products that enhance others, saves catalog space and an easy upsell
       Customizable Product: Offer single product with configurations
      Subscriptions: convenience is the product!

    Develop one product before a line. Preorders? Even non-sales helps data.
    Traffic: requires an investment, many methods(time/money/both)
      Advertising: quick results, many options, learning curve, cost to use service
      Content Marketing: create content on site to educate visitors
      Social Media: megaphone for existing content, can reach people who aren’t searching

    Increasing Customer Lifetime Value: Bulk offers, newsletters, upselling

    High Quality Products return customers without encouragement, higher R&D, production, and branding
    Consumable products have huge opportunity for returning company
    Social Media: Subtle reminder to return, and provide content beyond ads
    Newsletters: Remind customers of sales and collects emails for info
    Marketing Automation: automated emails to target specific customer types

    Increasing Average Order Value (AOV):
    Premium products: no price cap,
    Price anchoring: congitive bias to rely on first piece of info they see ($6K TV next to $600 tv)
    Cross-sell: promoting similar/related product Tiered pricing: discounts at higher totals to encourage more spending

    GETTING PAID
    Website: Add products to cart and checkout ->
    Payment Gateway: Check if funds are available [Traditional: req. Merchant account][Modern: authorizes and draws funds]Authorize charge: checking if funds, capture a charge: obtain the payment
    Payment acceptance: Paypal safer than credit cards, usually included in Wordpress Plus What country/currency? Risk?(Credit card #s)
    shipping/billing address =>Off-site confirmation
    Recurring payments may be unallowed, difficult to integrate with off-site gateways, or inflexible
    Separate shopping cart or off-site version?
    Paypal and Stripe are good off-site gateways

    Shipping costs
    free/flat rate/estimates
    Digital downloads (no shipping)
    Flat rates (x per order + y per item)
    Get estimates (more difficult to set up)
    Table of Rates (Complex setup+test, uses weight and # and type of items) anything beyond flat rate will take time to setup

    Order processing, refunding unavailable orders

    Multiple sites has larger marketing area Using off-site account: rare feedback, contact info unshared, large customer base but difficult to manage inventory across multiple sites

    Taxes: Federal, State, County, City
    Nexus: physical presence in a place
    Tax-inclusive or exclusive ($10+)
    “Sleek” checkouts only need email address and may not be legal everywhere
    Tax exemption: seek a tax professional
    TAXAMO helps with taxes as well as GUMROAD

    Security Unsecured traffic can be intercepted, All sensitive information should be secured
    Secure your site to collect payment details
    Installing SSL certificate (many hosts do this for small fee)
    PCI DDS compliance can be demanding
    OUTLINE SSL SECURITY PLAN FOR SITE
    Options: Clef, Wordpress 3rd party.

    Freemium platform Core functionality free, but advanced/niche funct. Paid Platform extensions, official and unofficial, available WooCommerce, iThemesExchange, WPeCommerce Ecommerce incredibly complex-choose developer reputation over features
