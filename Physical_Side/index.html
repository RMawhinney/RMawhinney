SUBJECT: Physical Side

While not necessarily the best point to begin from, it is at least good to know where you may end up with a career in Web Technologies. It is not entirely wise to begin a journey without at least some idea of the Final Destination, and the Web Technologies course helps better acclimate us towards understanding the requirements for said destination. The main consideration for this section is opportunity. For that purpose this section goes over the details briefly of a few of the kind of venues that are available within the Fresno area, as well as the different criteria and basic components that a wide category of positions would be interacting with and responsible over during their business. There is a short section on careers, as well as a topic of Full-Stack vs Front End, a comparison of the requirements for most Web Technology / Development-related fields.
