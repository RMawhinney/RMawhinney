TOPIC: Careers

My search parameters were for "web development wanted fresno" on Google.

My first find was a local company called Digital Attic (Links to an external site.). Their services include creative design, development, video, marketing, and programming, among other things. They boast over 5000 projects among 581 clients, and have been in business since 1992. Their clientele has included SaveMart, Sun-Maid, PG&E and Jeep, and assisted organizations like Fresno County Fire, Fresno Rescue Mission and the Bulldog Foundation. They expect knowledge about PHP, PHP MVC framework, MySQL, Javascript, jQuery, AJAX and HTML5/CSS3 as a baseline for back-end developing.

The second site I found was Worldlight Media (Links to an external site.), a strategic branding + web design company. They offer several different site templates to start with and have a wide spanning clientele as well. They offer small internship opportunities as well.

The last I chose was CreativeCali (Links to an external site.). Admittedly, they operate mainly out of Sacramento but they have offered search engine optimization, web design, and marketing services since 2009.

Each of these sites had very top-of-the-line graphics and intuitive layouts to be expected from their expertise. Each one had multiple social media outlets to be contacted through and looked very professional. Just with these three examples I can see how widespread and ubiquitous web development is and how it is in such high demand. While I wasn't able to find anything pertaining specifically to salaries some did offer good benefits.
